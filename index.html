I am now serving as a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Interactive Digital Media at La Trobe University, Australia. Before joining La Trobe, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Expanded Perception and Interaction Centre (EPICentre), UNSW and a visiting scientist at CSIRO's Data61. I earned my PhD degree in Information Systems Technology and Design from Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), where I was advised by Prof Sai-Kit Yeung and co-advised by Prof Lap-Fai Yu. I received my BSc degree in GIS from Zhejiang University (ZJU). I was a visiting scholar at UMass Boston during my PhD study.

I received generous support from CSIRO, DST Group, UNSW and SUTD.

My research focuses on AI-based Data-driven Techniques for Computational Design, Graphics and Vision, especially about Urban Modelling and Percpetion.
